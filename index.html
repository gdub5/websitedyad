The Challenge

The AI Investment Gap Is Real

Nearly 9 in 10 leaders have invested in AI for customer service. Only about 1 in 10 have achieved mature deployment that delivers real value.

The technology works. AI capabilities have arrived. So why aren't you seeing results?

Organizational Readiness

Most companies aren't prepared for what AI implementation actually requires. It's not a software rollout. It's a transformation.

The Leadership-Team Gap

Leadership measures deployment speed and automation rates. Teams experience unclear processes, inadequate training, and tools that don't fit their workflows.

Change Management Deficit

Half of executives say change management is their primary barrier to AI value. Yet most budgets don't account for it.

You don't need better AI. You need to close the gap between AI potential and organizational reality.
The Solution

Bridging the Gap Between AI Investment and AI Value

Dyad CX focuses on what technology vendors ignore: the organizational factors that determine whether AI actually works.

Identify the Gaps

We pinpoint exactly what's blocking AI success in your operation. Not generic assessments. Specific, actionable insights.

Build Readiness

We work alongside your team to create the organizational readiness that makes implementation stick. Training, process design, change management.

Drive Measurable Results

We define success metrics upfront and track them throughout. Customer experience, team productivity, service quality. Real outcomes, not vanity metrics.

This isn't about choosing the right AI platform. It's about making sure your organization can actually use it.

Services

How We Work With You

Every engagement starts with understanding your specific situation. We offer structured partnerships that scale with your needs, from initial assessment to full transformation.

AI Readiness Assessment

1 week

Get clarity before committing to implementation. We analyze your current state, identify what's blocking AI success, evaluate organizational readiness, and deliver a prioritized roadmap with realistic timelines and clear success metrics.

Ideal for: Leaders who've invested in AI without seeing results, or who want to understand what success will require before committing resources.

Implementation Partnership

8 weeks

Prove the model with real results before full rollout. Over 8 weeks, we establish baselines, deploy initial AI capabilities with proper support, train your team on new processes, and document measurable wins that justify broader investment.

Ideal for: Organizations ready to demonstrate AI value with a focused pilot before committing to full transformation.

Full Transformation Partnership

4 months

Transform your support operation and achieve mature AI deployment. We guide you through assessment, implementation, team training, process redesign, and optimization. You finish with a team capable of ongoing management and clear ROI on your AI investment.

Ideal for: Organizations ready to move from surface-level automation to mature deployment that delivers measurable value.

Ongoing Optimization

Monthly

Continuous improvement after implementation. Monthly performance reviews, team coaching, quarterly strategic planning, and access to emerging best practices. We help you expand what's working and adapt as your needs evolve.

Ideal for: Organizations that have implemented AI and want continuous improvement and expansion.

About

Built From Both Sides

I'm Greg, founder of Dyad CX.

Over 18 years, I've led AI implementation, support operations, and product management for B2B SaaS and fintech companies. I've been the support leader trying to adopt new technology and frustrated when it didn't work. I've been the product manager building tools and watching implementations fail.

That dual perspective revealed a consistent pattern: the gap between AI capability and organizational readiness. Technology vendors sell the capability. Nobody helps with the readiness.

I work at the intersection of Support, Product, and Engineering. I'm comfortable debugging APIs, designing workflows, and presenting to executives. I build systems that work and help teams deliver.
